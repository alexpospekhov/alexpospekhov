# Alex Pospekhov

## Founder at [hyperfocus.tech](https://hyperfocus.tech) | System that runs social commerce operations

20+ years in tech, 5+ in space systems and frontier tech. I come from an industry where "we'll fix it in prod" gets people killed. You learn to obsess over data integrity, real-time visibility, and margins of error.

Then I started talking to e-commerce founders running $10M+ in revenue. They were pulling numbers from five different dashboards into a spreadsheet held together by prayer. I couldn't unsee the chaos.

That's why I founded **[hyperfocus.tech](https://hyperfocus.tech)** — system that runs your social commerce operations. Multiply your team's capacity. Unlock entirely new revenue streams. For growth-obsessed brands and the agencies that run them. My ADHD brain literally cannot track a manual spreadsheet without losing itself, so I built an AI system that does it flawlessly. We replace manual ops work so founders can focus on growth.
